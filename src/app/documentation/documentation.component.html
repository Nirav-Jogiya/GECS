Economic Overview

Total Supply: 1,0000,000 GECS

Voting Contract: 500,000 GECS

Every proposal releases 100 GECS to voting participants. Their reward is based on their understanding and support of the GECS Ecosystem using our incentivised weighting algorithm.

Uniswap Pool: 400,000 GECS

Owning GECS increases your voting weight and gives you a share of revenues generated from GECS developments.

Locked Developer Reserver: 100,000 GECS

3333 GECS are released every month to GECS developers. As the protocol matures, GECS participants will be able to vote on who and what this resource should be spent on.
